About me

HI! I have a wide spectra of knowledge within new media. I am a Graphic Designer and Web Developer. I have a burning desire for both web and print.

I have been working and produced almost everything from external print promotions with a budget of millions to advanced database solutions for the web and almost everything inbetween.

If I have to put one job title on myself I guess I would call myself a passionated general specialist within new media. I love to do it all, but my heart beats a little bit extra when it comes to interactive things - where design strengthen the functions and functions strengthen the design.

I have designed and created everything from logotypes, graphic profiles, catalogues, bags, posters in all various formats, flyers, invitations, brochures, packaging, stickers, web sites, CMS, newsletters and other various digital applications.

And I am usually working all the way from the idea to the final product.

Apart from the design work, as mentioned above, I am doing a lot of programming, both for front-end and back-end solutions. I am used to create and develop back-end solutions with databases and I have created various web applications with back-end solutions.

I am currently working, since five years, as Graphic Designer and Web Developer Nordic at GameStop. GameStop is an international company and the world's largest game retailer with over 6500 stores world wide. I am highly experienced within my working position and I have been active in new media, in various ways, in over 15 years.

Beside creating beautiful things with great functions I am a proud father who enjoy to be social, working out, adrenaline, humor, art, laughing and make people laugh and surrounding myself with lovely people!

I really hope you find this interesting, hear from you soon!
